\begin{equation}
    \Delta n_d=\frac{\delta slope}{slope}\times N_d
\end{equation}
and error in $V_{bi}$ is
\begin{equation}
    \Delta V_{bi}=V_{bi}\sqrt{\left(\frac{\delta slope}{slope}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\delta intercept}{intercept}\right)^2}
